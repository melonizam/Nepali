Controlling

Concept of control

Controlling is one of the major functions of management. Controlling function of management ensures the optimum utilization of organizational resources. Controlling is the process of measuring actual performance with standards. If there is any deviation, taking corrective measures. Hence, controlling function of management guides and regulates the activities of the employees.
Thus, controlling ensures the smooth operation of an organization and comparing it with the predetermined standards.
