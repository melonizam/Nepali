Communication

Concept of communication

Communication refers to the transference of ideasm, facts, figures, opinions and emotions from one person to anothers. It constitutes a scientific approach of telling, listening, conveying and understanding of messages.
According to Newman and Summer, “Communication is an exchange of facts ideas, opinios or emotions by two or more persons.”
Hence, communication is the process of the transference and understanding of meaning between the parties. Communication enables organizations to function effectively and efficiently.

Characteristic of communication

1. Two-way process
2. At least two individual
3. Message
4. Channel of communication
5. Pervassive in nature
6. Indispensible component

The process of communication

1. Sender/source has as idea/message
2. Sender encodes the idea/message
3. Sender transmits message
4. Receiver gets a message/idea
5. Receiver decodes the message
6. Receiver sends feedback
