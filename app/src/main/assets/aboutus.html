About MeroSpark

MeroSpark is defining the new era of education in Nepal. This app is designed to provide reference notes of different Academic level such as SLC, HSEB, ENGINEERING, CTEVT.Beside that we inform important events, notice which are published by associated educational department.For now this app only provides HSEB notes but in future we will expand our Notes library.We want to go hand in hand with students So, please feel free to give feedback.
